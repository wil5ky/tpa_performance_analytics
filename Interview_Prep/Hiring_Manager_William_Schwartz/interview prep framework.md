# Interview Prep Framework

## Motivational Fit
These questions are intended to gain insight into the candidate's aspirations and motivations for Zurich and the role.

**What is it that attracts you most to a role or career at Zurich?**
*Probes:*
* What is it about working for Zurich that excites you the most?
* Which aspects of our culture and values do you align most strongly with?

**What is it that most interests you about this role?**
*Probes:*
* Which aspects of the role do you think will energize and motivate you the most?
* What does this role offer that most interests you?
* Are there any aspects of the role that you might dislike or motivate you less?

**Scoring Guide:**
*Indicator:*
* Provides compelling reasons and rationale for wanting to join Zurich
* Articulates an understanding of the role and why it is of interest to them.
* Articulates motivations and interests that align with the realities of the role and context
* Has considered what might be less motivating or engaging about the role

**Motivational Fit Rating:** (1-5)

## Collaborate Together
Listen, communicate and build trust, Collaborate, influence and build alignment through partnership; Recognize own capabilities, limitations and impact on others; Embrace diversity of people and perspectives.

**Tell me about a time you succeeded in a project / initiative by collaborating with others.**

*Situation / Task*
* What was the objective of the project / initiative?
* Why was it important to collaborate?

*Action*
* How did you approach it?
* How did you monitor the team progress?

*Result*
* What impact did this have on the project / initiative and its timelines?
* What did you take away from this?

**Describe a time when you did not achieve your individual goals because you did not to ask for help from others.**

*Situation / Task*
* What were you setting out to achieve?
* What did you find most challenging about asking for help?

*Action*
* What approach did you take to achieve your individual goal?
* Why did you choose to do it that way?

*Result*
* What was the result?
* On reflection, what would you do if faced with this kind of situation again?

**Scoring Guide:**
*Indicator:*
* Provides clear direction and accountabilities
* Delegates and distributes assignments and decisions appropriately
* Effective utilisation of others' talents and energy to achieve results
* Delegates authority to match role and responsibility

**Overall Rating:** (1-5)

## Develop & Grow
Show care and concern for people, Create motivating development opportunities for self & others; Build technical skills & capabilities; Provide feedback, praise & recognition.

**Describe actions you took that contributed to your personal or professional development.**

*Situation / Task*
* Please describe the background to this situation.
* What were you setting out to achieve?

*Action*
* How do you decide which are the most useful developmental activities for you?
* How did you monitor the progress of your development?

*Result*
* How successful were you in reaching your developmental goal?
* What did you take away from this?

**Tell me about a time when you identified an area of improvement and proactively sought out opportunities to develop it.**

*Situation / Task*
* What was the area of improvement?
* Why was it important to develop it?

*Action*
* How did you identify this area of improvement?
* What development approach did you take?
* Did you have the chance to apply what you learned on the job? How?

*Result*
* What was the result?
* On reflection, what would you have done differently?

**Tell me about a time you received negative feedback on an aspect of your work.**

*Situation / Task*
* Please describe the background to this situation.
* What triggered the feedback?

*Action*
* What was your reaction to the feedback?
* How did you feel?
* What did you do?

*Result*
* What did you find most difficult about the feedback?
* What did you realize about yourself?

**Scoring Guide:**
*Indicator:*
* Is clear in what they would like to achieve as part of their personal or professional development, with measurable objectives in place.
* Provides evidence of creating development opportunities to motivate self
* Places concerted effort into building own technical skills and capabilities
* Actively seeks feedback to identify areas of development, with targeted actions for improvement

**Competency Rating:** (1-5)

## Puts Customers First
Champion customer focus across the organization; Anticipate changes in the external environment; Address customer needs proactively; Ensure an outstanding customer experience

* What were the customer needs?

*Action*
* How did you go about planning for it?
* What considerations did you make?
* How did you ensure you provided the best possible service?

*Result*
* How successful were you?
* What was the impact on the customer?

**Tell me about a time when you obtained up-to-date information related to customers about the external environment (e.g. market landscape, regulatory environment, and competitor activity) and what you did with it?**

*Situation / Task*
* What was the situation?
* Where did you obtain this information from?
* What was this information about?

*Action*
* How did you decide what information was relevant?
* What did you do with the information?
* Why did you choose to do it that way?

*Result*
* What was the result?
* What was the impact on the customer?

**Tell me about a time when you found a customer problem especially difficult to deal with.**

*Situation / Task*
* What was the situation / challenge?
* What made it difficult to deal with this customer problem?

*Action*
* How did you approach it?
* What did you find most challenging about it?

*Result*
* What was the result?
* What have you learnt about how to address such interactions?

**Scoring Guide:**
*Indicator:*
* Demonstrates evidence of championing customer focus across the organisation
* Proactively anticipates changes in the external environment and their impact on the customer
* Identifies and addresses customer needs proactively
* Places considerable emphasis on ensuring customers receive outstanding experiences

**Competency Rating:** (1-5)

*Result*

* What was the outcome?

**Tell me about a situation which demonstrates your ability to solve a problem in a creative manner.**

*Situation / Task*

## Provide Clear Direction
Set clear & challenging business objectives; Keep self & others informed and connected to Zurich's strategic direction; Actively engage & support others through change; Ensure clarity in the role individuals and teams are playing

**Describe a time when it has been important for you to identity and set yourself challenging and personal business goals which are aligned to team and business goals.**

*Situation / Task*
* What approach did you adopt to ensure alignment with business goals?
* How did you ensure the clarity and input of others in defining these goals?

*Action*
* What internal and external impact did these goals have on the business?
* What outcome did focusing on these goals have towards driving change?

*Result*
* What was the result of your actions?
* What learning did you take on board from the situation?

**Tell me about a time you motivated a colleague who felt detached from the larger organization.**

*Situation / Task*
* What was the situation?
* How did you know this colleague felt detached from the larger organization?

*Action*
* How did you help this colleague connect to Zurich's strategic direction?
* What challenges did you face and how did you overcome them?

*Result*
* What impact did your actions have on this colleague?
* On reflection, what would you have done differently?

**Describe a time when your work goals were impacted by a colleague or team member who was not meeting expectations.**

*Situation / Task*
* What were the circumstances of this situation?
* What information did you consider when deciding how to deal with this scenario?

*Action*
* Describe the steps you took to resolving this issue
* How did you evaluate and follow up in this situation?

*Result*
* What impact did your actions have on your team member's performance? Why?
* What learning did you gain from this situation?

**Scoring Guide:**
*Indicator:*
* Demonstrates effective setting of clear and challenging business objectives
* Ensures self and others are kept up to date and connected to Zurich's strategic direction
* Actively sets business goals that support and promote change
* Recognizes interdependencies amongst others to achieve common work goals, taking a shared responsibility approach

**Competency Rating:** (1-5)

## Make it Happen
Act decisively and courageously in the face of ambiguity; Enable & energize other to deliver sustainable results; Hold self and empower others to be accountable for results; Show and set expectations for excellence in execution.

**Tell me about a time when you had to make a decision and the information available was inadequate.**

*Situation / Task*
* Describe briefly the situation that faced you
* Why was this necessary to make the decision even though the information available was insufficient?

*Action*
* What factors did you consider when making the decision?
* Did you involve anyone else in the decision-making process? If yes, who did you involve?
* Why did you choose to approach it this way?

*Result*
* What was the outcome of your decision?
* What did you learn from this experience?

**Please give me an example that demonstrates your approach to monitoring the progress of team members and holding them accountable.**

*Situation / Task*
* What was the situation?
* What was the objective of the task the team was working on?

*Action*
* How did you ensure others followed through on their commitments?
* Describe the steps you took to monitor the progress of work?
* What was your reaction when someone did not meet his/her commitments?

*Result*
* What was the result?
* What did you take away from this?

**Tell me about a time you were unable to follow through on a commitment.**

*Situation / Task*
* What were the circumstances of this situation?
* What made it challenging for you to follow through on the commitment?

*Action*
* What steps did you take to follow through on the commitment?
* To what extent do you feel you tried to follow through on the commitment?

*Result*
* What was the outcome?
* What learning did you gain from this situation?

**Scoring Guide:**
*Indicator:*
* Acts decisively and demonstrates courage in ambiguous situations
* Demonstrates the ability to actively enable and energize self to deliver sustainable results
* Is responsible and self-driven to achieve own results and empowers others to be accountable
* Clearly shows and sets expectations for excellence when executing actions and plans

**Competency Rating:** (1-5)

*Indicator:*
