At Zurich North America Claims we acknowledge that work life-balance and
flexibility are a priority when it comes to choosing your next career move.
Designed with our employees' needs in mind, the ZNA Claims hybrid work
model emphasizes flexibility, allowing claims employees to conduct
individual work in their preferred location, while facilitating in-person

While the model provides a high level of flexibility and autonomy, occasional
circumstances requiring in-office attendance should be expected. This is a
hybrid role within the U.S., preferred assigned location is Schaumburg, IL but
open to considering alternate Zurich U.S. office locations for a well-qualified
candidate.
Are you passionate about turning data into actionable insights? This role
focuses on analyzing complex datasets to uncover trends and drive
strategic decisions, while also managing the downstream flow of TPA data
through Claims Mart—ensuring data quality, understanding actuarial
impacts, and resolving data flow issues. You'll create visual workflows,
evaluate performance, and deliver impactful recommendations to
stakeholders.
In this role you will be responsible for:
As the first point of contact for all strategic initiatives for a region or
segment, provide advanced financial analysis and modeling.
Serve as the point of contact for any supporting external consultancies
when needed to assist with business performance reviews, including
in-depth analyses in specific areas.
Lead the preparation of key messages, reports and financial analyses
to inform management teams of expected outcomes for specific
initiatives.
Lead the production of periodic benefit follow-ups for key initiatives
within the relevant region or segment to ensure financial targets are
met.
Develop and maintain the governance framework to ensure that all
business initiatives are subject to high-quality, systematic financial
scrutiny.
Prepare analyses and comments for all reporting requirements of the
Group's projects and be the first point of contact for all inquiries
related to financial projections to ensure that all project outputs are
obtained.
Lead the production of profitability reports on behalf of the region or
segment, monitoring performance trends by product or line of
business to identify any substantially underperforming or
outperforming.
Lead the development of new reporting initiatives for the region or
segment with the goal of providing additional and deeper insight into
performance trends to improve decision-making.
